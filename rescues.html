Please note.

Our adoption fees include age appropriate vaccinations, deworming, spay/neuter, microchipping, and testing for feline aids (FIV) and feline leukemia (FeLV). KAWS only covers the vaccinations up until the point that the animal is adopted. We make sure the animal is up-to-date on their vaccinations before they are adopted, but anything after that is the adopter's responsibility.

We firmly believe that cats should not be allowed to roam freely outdoors due to the dangers from poison, to vehicles and wild animals. If you wouldn't let your dog roam your neighbourhood, then why would you let your cat? Instead, we recommend that you train your cat to walk on a harness and leash or build them their own outdoor enclosure. We realize that some cats cannot be happily confined, so if you let them outdoors, please be responsible and have them wear a collar and tag.

* Adoption fee for kittens under 6 months of age consists of a $120.00 adoption fee plus an $80.00 refundable spay/neuter deposit.
